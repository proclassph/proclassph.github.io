What Is Fake News?


There are two kinds of fake news:


1. Stories that aren't true. These are entirely invented stories designed to make people believe something false, to buy a certain product, or to visit a certain website.


2. Stories that have some truth, but aren't 100 percent accurate. For example, a journalist quotes only part of what a politician says, giving a false impression of their meaning. Again, this can be deliberate, to convince readers of a certain viewpoint, or it can be the result of an innocent mistake. Either way, it quickly attracts an audience and can become entrenched as an "urban myth."

WHAT ARE YOUR PRIMARY BELIEFS ABOUT LOVE?

Is there such a thing as true love? And, if that's the case, what common beliefs do people have in order to build such a lovely, harmonious union? Divorce is on the rise nowadays, with approximately one divorce for every marriage. Many people marry without having a strong inner basis.  so we walk around seeking for the love of our lives rather than leaping in and being it. Most people believe that all they have to do is follow their hearts in order to discover genuine love, and the rest will take care of itself. But it's crucial to know who you are before you fall in love. There are crucial ideas you must have to bring real love into your life. Believe that you are deserving of love that is unconditional. Your relationships are reflections of who you are. At work, it's the law of attraction. As a result, your connections are only as strong as your self-perception. You will receive unconditional love if you think you are deserving of it. It's crucial to love oneself completely, even if you have flaws. How can you expect someone else to love you entirely if you don't love yourself? You will when you learn to unconditionally love yourself, believe in your own merit, and remain open to receiving it from others.
