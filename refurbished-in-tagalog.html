2. Have an old furniture reupholstered or refurbished instead of buying new ones​


Explanation:

It depends on the situation:

a.) If you have enough money and that buying a new one is worth it with the furniture's quality and sturdiness compared to refurbishing and reupholstering (on your own or on fixing centers), then, go for buying a new one.

b.) If you plan to go thrift and your furniture is sturdy and just needs a little fixing, then you fix it on your own or have it fixed to fixing centers, though it takes time for it to look new again.
