8. A bracelet and a watch together cost P 3,000. The watch costs P 2, 000 more than the bracelet. What are the cost of the bracelet and the cost of the watch?


Answer:

Bracelet= P1000

Watch = P2000

Step-by-step explanation:

Bracelet and watch=3000

x is bracelet

3000=x+2000

3000-2000=x

1000=x

Representation:

Let x = cost of the bracelet

Let x + 2000 = cost of the watch

3000 = total cost

Working Equation:

x + x + 2000 = 3000

Solution:

x + x + 2000 = 3000

2x = 3000 - 2000

2x = 1000

2x ÷ 2 = 1000 ÷ 2

x = 500 (price of the bracelet)

500 + 2000 = 2500 (cost of the watch)

Answer:

The bracelet cost P500 while the watch cost P2500.

10. Tatiana wants to give friendship bracelets to her 32 classmates. She already has 5 bracelets, and she can buy more bracelets in packages of 4.


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

If she buys 5 packages, she will add 5*4 = 20 to the 5 she already has for a total of 25. That is not enough since she need at least 32.  

She has 5, she needs 32 or 32-5 = 27 more. If n is the number of packages she must buy, then:

4n + 5 ≥ 32

4n ≥ 27

n ≥ 27/4

n ≥ 6.75

The smallest integer greater than or equal to 6.75 is 7.

She must buy 7 more packages of 4 to have enough for all her classmates. 5 packages will not be enough
