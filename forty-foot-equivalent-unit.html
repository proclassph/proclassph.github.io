Celsius to Fahrenheit:

° F= ° C (9/5) + 32

° F= 40(9/5) + 32

° F= 72 + 32

° F= 104° F

Celsius to Kelvin:

° K= ° C + 273.15

° K= 40 +273.15

° K= 313.15 ° K
