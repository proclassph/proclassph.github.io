24. Find the cost per pound of chocolate coated corn flakes made from 40 pounds of chocolate which costs php200,00 per pound and 120 pounds of cornflakes which costs php88,00 per pound ​


Answer:

The cost per pound of chocolate coated corn flakes is Php116.00

Step-by-step explanation:

Cost of chocolate = 40 pounds x Php200.00/pound = Php8,000.00

Cost of cornflakes = 120 pounds x Php88.00/pound = Php10,560.00

Total cost of chocolate coated corn flakes = Cost of chocolate + Cost of cornflakes

= Php8,000.00 + Php10,560.00

= Php18,560.00

divide the total cost by the total weight of the chocolate coated corn flakes:

Total weight of chocolate coated corn flakes = 40 pounds + 120 pounds = 160 pounds

Cost per pound = Total cost of chocolate coated corn flakes / Total weight of chocolate coated corn flakes

= Php18,560.00 / 160 pounds

= Php116.00/pound

Therefore, the cost per pound of chocolate coated corn flakes is Php116.00.
