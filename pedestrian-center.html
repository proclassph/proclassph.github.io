Step-by-step explanation:

the total walking area available = the area of the park (circle with radius 700 m) - the are of the fountain (circle with radius 5 m)

the total walking area available = 700^2*pi - 5^2*pi

the total walking area available = (700^2 - 5^2)*pi

the total walking area available = 489975*pi

the total walking area available = 1,538,521.5 m^2

the total walking area available to pedestrians visiting the park is 1,538,521.5 m^2.

18. A circular fountain with a 5-m radius lies along the center of a circular park with a 70-m radius. Calculate the total walking area available to pedestrians visiting the park


Answer:

15,315.25 square meters

Computation:

Given,

[tex]\left[\begin{array}{ccc}Circular\:fountain:&5\:m&radius\\Circular\:park:&70\:m&radius\end{array}\right][/tex]

Determination of areas.

[tex]\boxed{Area\:of\:the\:circular\:fountain}\\A=\pi r^{2}=(3.14159...)(5\:m)^{2}=(3.14159)(25\:m^{2})=78.53975\:m^{2}\\\\\boxed{Area\:of\:the\:circular\:park}\\A=\pi r^{2}=(3.14159...)(70\:m)^{2}=(3.14159)(4,900\:m^{2})=15,393.791\:m^{2}[/tex]

Total walking area available to pedestrians visiting the park.

[tex]Area\:of\:circular\:park-Area\:of\:circular\:fountain\\15,393.791\:m^{2}-78.53975\:m^{2}=15,315.25125\:m^{2} \approx\boxed{15,315.25m^{2}}[/tex]
