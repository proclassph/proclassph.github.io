Answer:

The theme of the story "The Hunger in Barok" is the effects of poverty and hunger on individuals and their communities. It depicts the struggles of the people in Barok, a poor village in the Philippines, to survive and feed themselves amidst a lack of resources and opportunities. The story also shows how the hunger and poverty can lead people to commit desperate acts, such as stealing or resorting to violence, and how it can affect their relationships and mental health. The theme of the story ultimately highlights the need for greater social and economic support for impoverished communities.
