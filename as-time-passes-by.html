1. In a class of 30 students, 90% passed their subject on time. How many students of the class passed their project on time? How many students were not able to pass the project on time?


Given that the class size is 30, 90% of the students passed their subject on time. So, the number of students who passed their project on time is 30 * 90% = 27 students.

The number of students who did not pass their project on time is 30 - 27 = 3 students.
