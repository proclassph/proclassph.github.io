4. Customer Brenda books a 3-hour cleaning and was charged $94.50. The customer wants to apply a 2-hour voucher for the recently completed cleaning and get a refund. How much should the customer be refunded(TOTAL amount)?


PROBLEM SOLVING

[tex]__________________________[/tex]

Problem:

A customer named Brenda books a 3-hour cleaning and was charged $94.50. She wants to apply a 2-hour voucher for the recently completed cleaning and get a refund. How much should the customer be refunded?

What is asked:

How much should the customer be refunded?

Given:

$94.5 for a 3-hour cleaning

Answer & Solution:

First, we have to find how much does a 1-hour cleaning cost by dividing 94.5 by 3 which will result to 31.5.

$94.5 ÷ 1 hour = $31.5 per hour

Second, multiply 31.5 by 2 which results to 63 to identify how much does a 2-hour cleaning cost

31.5 x 2 = $63 for 2 hours

↬ Hence, the customer should receive a refund of $63.
