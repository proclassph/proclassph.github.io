17. Explain how these 3 major points causes the miseries of the Filipinos during Spanish Era according to Dr. Rizal 1. Implementation of Military Policies2. Deterioration and disappearance of the Filipino indigenous culture 3. Passivity and submissiveness to the Spanish colonizers​


Answer:

1. The implementation of military policies by the Spanish colonizers led to the misery of the Filipinos during that era. These policies included the enslavement of the native population, the forced labor in the mines and plantations, and the imposition of taxes that were often impossible to pay.

2. The deterioration and disappearance of the Filipino indigenous culture also caused misery for the Filipinos during the Spanish era. The Spanish colonizers discouraged the practice of native customs and beliefs, and instead forced the Filipinos to adopt Spanish language and culture. This led to a loss of identity and a feeling of inferiority among the Filipinos.

3. The passivity and submissiveness of the Filipinos to the Spanish colonizers also caused misery during that era. The Filipinos were often too afraid to stand up to the Spanish authorities, and this led to a lack of progress and opportunities for the Filipino people.
