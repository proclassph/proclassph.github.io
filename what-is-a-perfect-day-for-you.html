18. i will have done the ironing by the time you get home what aspect of the verb is will have done ? A.simple aspect b progressive C. perfect D. perfect progressiveTHANK YOU & HAVE A NICE DAY​


I will have done the ironing by the time you get home. What aspect of the verb is will have done?

A. Simple aspect

B. Progressive

C. Perfect

D. Perfect Progressive

Actually, the aspect of the verb will have done is Future Perfect Tense, but Option C is the closest one. It cannot be a simple aspect since we use "will have" It is used to form a Future Perfect Tense. it also cannot be progressive aspect since from the verb itself "done" is already in it's past tense. It cannot be perfect progressive since the formula for perfect progressive is "have/has + been + present participle of the verb" For Example: has been cooking/have been dancing.

So the closest answer is C. Perfect. But it is really a Future Perfect Tense.
