6. Analyn borrowed an amount of p25,000 with a simple interest of 3. 5%. She promised to return the money at the end of 1 year and half. How much interest will analyn pay at the end of the period?​.


Answer:

IDENTIFY THE PROBLEM

Step-by-step explanation:

STEP 1.  identify the problem or what is asked?

   Answer is THE TOTAL AMOUNT OF INTEREST ANALYN WILL PAY ATE THE END OF THE PERIOD

STEP 2: IDENTIFY THE GIVEN:

PRINCIPAL AMOUNT IS PHP 25,000

RATE IS 3.5 %

TIME IS 1.5 YR

STEP 3: FIND THE FORMULA OOF SIMPLE INTEREST COMPUTATION

IS --------------

          A = P ( 1 + rt) where :

                                Formula

                                            A = P (1 + rt)

                                            A = final amount

                                            P = initial principal balance

                                            r = annual interest rate

                                            t = time (in years)

STEP 4: SOLVE

       A = P (1 + rt)

           = 25,000 ( 1 + ( 0.035 ) (1.5 ) -- NOTE 3.5% IN DECIMAL IS 0.035

           = 25,000 ( 1 + 0.0525 )  ---------NOTE 0.035 X 1.5 IS 0.0525

           = 25,000 ( 1.0525 )   ------------ NOTE ADD 1 + 0.0525

           = 26,312.50 ---------------NOTE MULTIPLY 25,000 BY 1.0525

STEP 5: SO WE ABLE TO GET THE TOTAL AMOUNT ANALYN WILL PAY AFTER 1.5 YR BUT TAKE NOTE THAT THE PROBLEM IS ASKING ONLY THE AMOUNT OF INTEREST SHE WILL PAY NOT THE TOTAL AMOUNT SHE WILL PAY INCLUDING THE PRINCIPAL

SO, WE WILL SUBRACT : 26, 312.50 - 25000 = 1,312.50

SO THE ANSWER IS PHP 1,312.50.

Answer:

Stand directly in front of the meter so that you can clearly see the location of each pointer.

If the pointer is between two numbers, read the number the pointer has just passed.

If the pointer is between 9 and 0, always read 9.

If the pointer appears to be exactly on a number, read the next lowest number unless the pointer to its right has passed zero. Since the first dial has no dial to its right, the number must be read independently.

20. Dina is now 18 years old and her colleague Analyn is 14 years old. How many years ago was Dina twice as old as Analyn?


The number of years passed when Dina's age is twice as Analyn's: x

Dina's present age: 18 ⇒ Dina's age x years ago: 18-x

Analyn's present age: 14 ⇒ Analyn's age x years ago: 14-x

Equation:

18 - x = 2(14-x)

18 - x = 28 - 2x

2x - x = 28 - 18

x = 10

ANSWER: 10 years ago.

Check:

Ages 10 years ago:

Dina: 18-10 = 8 years old

Analyn: 14-10 = 4 years old

8 = 2(4)

8 = 8 (True)

HERE ARE THE ANSWER;

Analyn walks 800 meters and Daniele walks 8 kilometers. To compare the two, it is necessary to convert both distances to the same unit of measurement. One kilometer is equal to 1000 meters, so Daniele walks 8 * 1000 = 8000 meters

Now that both distances are in the same unit of measurement, it is clear that Daniele walks faster than Analyn because he walks a greater distance in the same amount of time.
