2. paki sagot mo sorry po pag na sayang kopo oras nyo​


Answer:

The seven parts of a flower are Pistil , Petal,  Stamen, Leaf  , Stem,  Receptacle and  Sepal

Explanation:

Pistil - One of the main function of a pistil is to produce ovule. It includes the female reproductive system of a flower as well as it receives the pollen from the stamen.

Petal - A petal protects the reproductive systems of a flower. It is visually served as attraction for a specific pollinators to the flower.

Stamen - Producing pollens are mainly its function. It usually comes with the slender filament supporting the anther.

Leaf - The leaf serves as the food producers of a flower.

Stem - The primary function of a stem is to support the leaves, petals and other parts of the flower. It is where the nutrients flows from the root to the different areas of the plant or flower.

Receptacle - One of its main function is to connect both the stalk of the flowers and as well as it support the flower.

Sepal - The sepal is a defensive organ that encloses and protects the developing reproductive structures of the flower.

Sa mga tuntunin ng pamamahala ng oras, naniniwala ako na napakahalaga na gamitin ang oras ng isang tao nang matalino at may layunin. Sa personal, gumagawa ako ng malay na pagsisikap upang matiyak na ang aking oras ay ginugugol nang makabuluhan. Natukoy ko ang ilang partikular na aktibidad na nagpapakita ng mahusay at matalinong paggamit ng aking oras, tulad ng pagtatakda ng mga partikular na layunin at pag-prioritize ng mga gawain. Upang matugunan ang isyung ito, plano kong limitahan ang aking paggamit sa mga platform na ito at sa halip ay tumuon sa mas produktibong aktibidad na makakatulong sa akin na makamit ang aking mga layunin.
