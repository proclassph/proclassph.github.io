14. grade 4 po (solve the following problems) mr. reyes bought 15 bags of cement at php120 each and 2 cubic meters of gravel at php530 per cubic meter. how much change will he get from the three php1000-peso bills?


Answer:

Mr. Reyes will get Php 140 exchange from the three Php 1000.

Step-by-step explanation:

[tex]120 \times 15 = 1800 [/tex]

[tex]530 \times 2 = 1060[/tex]

[tex]1800 + 1060 = 2860[/tex]

[tex]3000 - 2860 = 140[/tex]

Answer:

He will get 140 pesos change from his three 1000-peso bills.

Step-by-step explanation:

first, we have to figure out how much is the total cost of all the materials he bought.

since there are 15 bags of cement worth 120 each, we will multiply 15 by 120.

15 × 120 = 1800

therefore,he will pay 1800 pesos for 15 bags of cement.

he also bought 2 cubic meters of gravel worth 530 pesos per cubic meter, so we will multiply 2 by 530.

2 × 530 = 1060

he will pay 1060 pesos for 2 cubic meters of gravel.

now, we will figure out how much is the change he will get from three 1000-peso bills.

there are three 1000 pesos bills, which means

3 × 1000 = 3000

now, we will subtract his money to the total amount of the materials he bought.

1800 + 1060 = 2860

(this is the total expenses of Mr. Reyes, we added 1800—the cost of 15 bags of cement— by 1060—the cost of 2 cubic meters of gravel.)

3000 - 2860 = 140

therefore, he will get 140 pesos change from his three 1000-peso bills.
