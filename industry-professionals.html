5. what is the major sis role in of event industry professionals?​


Answer:

Event Tech Expert

An event tech expert is your go to person for every technology related element surrounding an event. This might range from audio visual related items and special effects, all the way up to something as complex as your event app or other event technology. Many events have really started to embrace the tech savvy side of the industry and at the same time many event planners have too much on their plate already. Event tech is sometimes a step too far and so some eventprofs are looking to work with a go-to person to advise on the best options from the start, ensuring that the tech is implemented properly and supporting and moderating as required on site.

Most event tech experts will spend their days troubleshooting any tech challenges that arise, but they will also play a critical role during your site visits and set up to be sure your venue is the perfect fit for all of the ideas you plan to implement.

Social Media Coordinator

22. Jargon is the language used by professionals from specific industries.


Jargon

Jargons are terms or words used by professionals in specific industries that only they can understand. They use jargon to convey messages with the use of terms that are not common to the public.

Importance of jargons

Sharing of identity with a common understanding is one of the main importance of using jargon. For example, the word virus for a doctor would have a different meaning for a web developer. It also creates a culture between professionals that shares and creates a common language.

Some jargon may have the same word between two or more professional industries but they have different meanings.

ExamplesVirus
The word virus in the Information Technology industry may mean malware or malfunction of the system applications but in the medical field, it is something that can cause health failures.

Order
In the law industry, the order is an issued order by the court or authority. In the food industry, it is something that is demanded by the customer to get from the store or establishment.

Court
In the sports industry, it means a place where activities or sports competition happens. The law industry defines it as a place where trials and legal proceedings are made.

Rest
For a musician, rest refers to the sudden stop in music which is different from the literal meaning we know.

Firewall
In the information technology and security industry, it is a protection against malware and viruses to avoid computer infections. In the architectural and engineering industry, this refers to the establishment boundary to avoid fire.

23. how important is it is continuing professional development in your industry?​


Answer:

Continuing professional development (CPD) is essential in many industries as it helps individuals to stay up-to-date with the latest advancements, technologies, and best practices in their field. Here are some reasons why CPD is important:

Stay competitive: In a rapidly evolving job market, it is important to stay competitive by keeping up with the latest trends and developments in your field. By continuously learning and developing new skills, you can remain relevant and attractive to employers.

Enhance job performance: CPD can help you develop new skills and knowledge that can enhance your job performance. This can lead to improved productivity, better quality work, and increased job satisfaction.

Career advancement: CPD can help you progress in your career by demonstrating your commitment to professional growth and development. This can lead to new job opportunities, promotions, and salary increases.

Networking opportunities: CPD can provide opportunities to network with other professionals in your industry, which can lead to new business partnerships, mentorship opportunities, and career connections.

Compliance and regulation: In some industries, CPD is mandatory for professionals to maintain their license or certification. Failure to comply with CPD requirements can result in disciplinary action or loss of credentials.

Overall, CPD is essential in many industries to stay competitive, enhance job performance, advance your career, build professional networks, and maintain compliance with industry regulations.
