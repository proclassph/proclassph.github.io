16. critical analysis essay​


Answer:

Critical analysis is the process of evaluating and interpreting information or an argument to form an independent judgment. It involves breaking down a text or argument into its component parts, examining each part in detail, and evaluating the argument's validity and reliability. The goal of critical analysis is to increase one's understanding of a subject and make informed decisions.

27. What is critical analysis


Critical Analysis is when you assess the success of a project thoroughly, considering what it does well and what it does poorly. We have to read the piece carefully and before writing your piece, you may need to look up words or ideas that you are unfamiliar with or research-related learning.

What does a critical analysis include?

Introduction:           Write the title of the work, author and the date of publication           State your own statement of thesis and your main work concept            Identify the main ideas of the book and the topic of the writer

    2. Summary:

            Briefly outline the novel, article or any topic's key ideas             Must include "who", "what", "where", "when", "why", and "how"             You can also decide on the design, theme or perspective

    3. Analysis:

                    The report includes a number of different sections.

            Specify what you like and don't like about the topic             Evaluate whether the writer has accomplished his intended objective             Explain your thoughts with a specific piece, article or topic             Analyze if the topic is:

                                         - Concentrated, easy to understand, credible, simple, detailed

                                         - New, innovative, well-written, and insightful

                                         - Targeted at a specific audience, fulfilling the intent

                                         - Well studied, with the best findings, and more ...

     4. Conclusion:

              Refresh your paper using new findings               If possible, describe your main ideas with fresh and more powerful terms              Call for action

It should be fun to consider critical analysis. This is your opportunity to say what you think of a piece, but with persuasive points and precise details from the text, you need to back up your opinions.

Their rival, Benigno Aquino, was imprisoned, exiled, and finally murdered, and his widow, Cory, was later to take over as president herself. There are wrenchingly emotional interviews with those who were detained and tortured under the Marcos regime. A grisly symbol of corruption is the bizarre wild animal reserve that Imelda created on Calauit Island, a vanity project that involved moving hundreds of families off the land, and whose giraffes are now not properly cared for like everyone and everything else.


29. patnubay sa paggawa ng critical analysis paper​


Upang lubos na makatulong sa pagharap sa hamon sa isyung pang-ekonomiya ay sumulat ng isang critical analysis paper tungkol sa isyung may kinalaman sa isa sa mga ito: Globalisasyon, Paggawa at Migrasyon. Ang suliranin o hamong pang-ekonomiyang susuriin ay batay sa mga impormasyon at datos na nakalap sa Gawain 5, 6, 8, 9 at 11 ng yunit na ito kaya naman inaasahan na ang mga suliraning susuriin ay batay sa iyong karanasan  ng mga kamag-aral. Siguruhing makapagmumungkahi ng mga posibleng solusyon sa suliraning sinuri.

Patnubay sa paggawa ng Critical Analysis Paper

I. Panimula (Introduction) - Maikling pagsasalaysay o paglalahad ng nilalaman ng Analysis Paper.

II. Suliraning Pang-ekonomiya (Economic Problem) - Paliwanag sa suliraning pang-ekonomiyang susuriin. Ilalahad dito ang salik at dahilan ng pag-usbong ng suliraning ito.

III. Paglalahad at pagsusuri ng datos (Analysis) - Paglalahad at pagsusuri ng mga datos. Ang mga datos ay batay sa mga impormasyong nakalap sa mga gawain ng yunit na ito.

IV. Konklusyon (Conclusion) - Pagbibigay konklusyon sa mga datos upang makabuo ng mga pahayag na magiging batayan ng solusyon.

V. Solusyon (Solution) - Kauukulang solusyon o alternatibo na makatutugon sa suliranin o hamong pang-ekonomiyang sinuri.
