In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebraic expression and basic mathematics especially in solving word problem about probability.

Problem 1

In 24 coin tossed, Harry got heads up 12 times. What is the experimental probability that Harry will make get tails up on his next toss?

Solution

P(A) = Number of favourable outcome (E) / Total Number of Favourable outcome (S)

P(A) = E/S

Probability of head = 12/24

Probability of head = 1/2

Probability of tail = 1 - probability of head

Probability of tail = 1 - 1/2

Probability of tail = 1/2, answer

Problem 2

Each of the letters in the word PHILIPPINES are in a separate card face down on a table. If you pick a card at random, what is the probability that its letter will come after letter O in the alphabet?

Solution

Letter after O are P (3) and S (1)

E = 4

S = 11

P(A) = 4/11, answer

Problem 3

Find the probability of getting a number less than 5 in a single throw of a die.

Solution

Less than 5 are 1, 2, 3 and 4

E = 4

S = 6

P(A) = 4/6

P(A) = 4/6 ÷ 2/2 = 2/3

P(A) = 2/3, answer

Problem 4

If a die is rolled once, what is the probability that it will show an even number?

Solution

Even number are 2, 4 and 6

E = 3

S = 6

P(A) = 3/6

P(A) = 3/6 ÷ 3/3 = 1/2

P(A) = 1/2, answer

Problem 5

A bag contains 5 white counters, 8 black counters and 7 green counters. What is the probability of drawing a green counter?

Solution

S = 5 + 8 + 7 = 20

E = 7

P(A) = 7/20, answer
