14. Isulat ang interval ng mga nota, 8. 9. 10. 高 11 12 13. 6 14. 。​


Answer:

8. 7th

9. unison

10. 3rd

11. 7th

12. 5th

13. 4th

14. 2nd

Explanation:

Answers:

1. measure 1 = unison

2. measure 2 = 3rd interval

Explanations:

Melodic Interval is the distance from one note to the other. In melodic interval, the notes are played one by one.

For you to know the distance you have to count from the first note on the left side to the note being ask.  In naming the distance we are using ordinal numbers.

Remember:

if the notes are on the same tone or pitch it is called unison.

if the distance is 2 - second or 2nd

if the distance is 3 - third or 3rd

if the distance is 4 - fourth or 4th

if the distance is 5 - fifth or 5th

if the distance is 6 - sixth or 6th

if the distance is 7 - seventh or 7th

if the distance is 8 – octave
