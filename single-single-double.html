13. which part of the court is for singles and doubles​


Answer:

In tennis, the court is divided into different parts for singles and doubles matches.

The singles court is narrower than the doubles court. The singles court measures 78 feet (23.77 meters) in length and 27 feet (8.23 meters) in width. The service line is located 21 feet (6.40 meters) from the net.

The doubles court is wider than the singles court. The doubles court measures 78 feet (23.77 meters) in length and 36 feet (10.97 meters) in width. The service line is located 21 feet (6.40 meters) from the net, just like in the singles court.

So, both singles and doubles matches can be played on the same court, but the court is wider for doubles matches.
