2. A 0.013 kg I.D. is made to whirl in a circular path at the end of the lanyard. What is the tangential velocity if the I.D. makes 2.5 revolutions in 2.5 seconds? What is centripetal acceleration? What is the tension in the lanyard?


Answer:

Tangential velocity = 2.5 revolutions/2.5 seconds = 1 revolution/second = 2π rad/s

Centripetal acceleration = v²/r = (2π rad/s)²/0.013 kg = 24,613.9 rad/s²

Tension in the lanyard = m·a = 0.013 kg·24,613.9 rad/s² = 321.77 N
