There is no one answer to this question as it depends on the specific situation and the laws of the jurisdiction in which the crime was committed. Generally speaking, minors who commit crimes are tried in a juvenile court system, which is designed to be more lenient and focused on rehabilitation than the adult court system. However, there may be cases in which a minor is tried as an adult, particularly if the crime is particularly serious.
