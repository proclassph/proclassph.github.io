1. Are you dependent on the Internet?

This question varies from people so this is in my case (And if your are like me, you can also use this as your answer). No. I am not entirely dependent on the Internet for I can continue my daily life even though detached from it. Having said that, there are parts of my life which is dependent on the internet. The academic and social aspect of me.

When studying (academic part), I can say I rely on internet with the evident searching I do in my school works, projects and school activities. Moreover, when teachers give vague lessons, I will depend on internet for tutorial videos especially on mathematics.

When socializing (social part), I cannot fully interact with people around me without the influence of internet especially social media. This is my avenue to communicate with friends and people even at home so I highly depend on it.

To sum up, I am not dependent on the internet. However, dependent on it only in academic and social matters. Not in biological ones such as eating, sleeping and living.

No. We cannot generalize them. But, there can be some digital natives who are truly dependent on the internet. It matters on how a person was raised. For example, a boy is a digital native since he was born in the abundance of technology yet raised in a farm where there is not much technology. He will grow not dependent from internet. Whereas, a boy in the city may be raised with gadgets and phones as his company. He can be truly dependent on the internet.

28. 3. When the dependent clause is at the beginning of the sentence, you need a _________ at the end of the dependent clause. ​


Answer:

When the dependent clause is at the beginning of the sentence,you need a comma at the end of the dependent clause

Explanation:

Putting a comma is important because it separates the two ideas of the sentence

Example: Since it was getting late, we decided to go

Independent clause: We decided to go

Dependent clause: Since it was getting late
