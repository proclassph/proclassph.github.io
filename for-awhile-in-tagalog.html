15. what would happen if your respiratory system stopped working for awhile​


Answer:

Even if it's for a while, you can't breathe without your respiratory system. It can result to death.

The normal exchange of oxygen and carbon dioxide in the lungs does not occur when a person develops acute respiratory failure. As a result, the heart, brain, and the rest of the body are deprived of sufficient oxygen. Shortness of breath, a bluish tint to the face and lips, and bewilderment are some of the symptoms.
