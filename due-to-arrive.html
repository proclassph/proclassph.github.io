Answer:

The total displacement is 53 km

Explanation:

Given:

A = 25 km East

B = 20 km 35° North of East

C = 35 km North

D = 10 km 20° North of West

S = ? (Total displacement)

Let x and y be the substitute for North, South, East, and West.

Vector A only moved 25 km to the East.

A = 25x + 0y

Vector B went 20 km 35° North of East. The 35° angle made the vector move vertically.

B = 20cos(35°)x + 20sin(35°) = 16x + 11y

Vector C only moved 35 km to the North.

C = 0x + 35y

Vector D went 10 km 20° North of West. We need to minus 10 km here since it went to the left.

D = - 10cos(20°) - 10sin(20°) = - 9x - 3y

Now we need to add the values of x's and y's

x = 25 + 20 + 0 - 9

x = 32

y = 0 + 11 + 35 - 3

y = 43

To solve for S we need to use this formula   [tex]S= \sqrt{x^2+y^2}[/tex]

[tex]S=\sqrt{32^2+43^2}[/tex]

[tex]S=\sqrt{1024+1849}[/tex]

[tex]S=\sqrt{2873}[/tex]

[tex]S= 53[/tex]

The total displacement of Mrs. Mendez is 53 km.
