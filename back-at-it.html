An earring backing that has a post that goes through a loop in the back is sometimes called the Omega Backing​

French back

The French Backing, also known as the "Omega" Backing, has a post that goes through a loop in the back. The French back is also loop of metal that hinges around the back of the ear and latches over the post where it emerges behind the earlobe to hold the earring in place.

A French clip is also type of earring backing that consists of an open, wire clip that fits over a post and against the earlobe. French clip-backed earrings are designed to keep the ear from bending downward at an unflattering angle. Omega backs get their name from their uncanny resemblance to the Greek letter omega. The ends of this looped finding attach to an earring via a hinge.
