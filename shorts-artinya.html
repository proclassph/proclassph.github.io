11. Marie bought 2 pairs of shorts and Ver P 2,000.00 on Monday. The next day be decided to buy her brothers 4 pairs of Shorts and e Shirts worth $4,600.00. How much is each Pair of short and shirt​


Answer:

To determine the cost of each pair of shorts and shirt, you need to first find the total cost of all the items Marie bought. The total cost of the items Marie bought is $2,000.00 for 2 pairs of shorts on Monday plus $4,600.00 for 4 pairs of shorts and 1 shirt on Tuesday, for a total of $6,600.00.

To find the cost of each pair of shorts, divide the total cost of the shorts by the number of pairs of shorts. In this case, the total cost of the shorts was $2,000.00 + $4,600.00 = $6,600.00 for 6 pairs of shorts. Dividing this amount by the number of pairs of shorts gives a cost of $6,600.00 / 6 pairs = $1,100.00 per pair of shorts.

To find the cost of each shirt divide the total cost of the items by the number of shirts. In this case, the total cost was $6,600.00 and there was 1 shirt, so the cost of each shirt was $6,600.00 / 1 shirt = $6,600.00 per shirt.

[tex]4x+3x=84[/tex]
