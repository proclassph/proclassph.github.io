13. 1. You push the wall. The equal but opposite reaction is____.A.the wall pushes you forwardB.the wall pushes you backwardC. the wall pushes you sidewardD. the wall pushes you downward​


Answer:

According to Newton's third law of motion, forces always act in equal but opposite pairs. Another way of saying this is for every action, there is an equal but opposite reaction. This means that when you push on a wall, the wall pushes back on you with a force equal in strength to the force you exerted.

15. a circular wall clock with a circumference of 8.5 is mounted on the wall how much are of the wall did occupy​


The area of the wall occupied by the circular clock would depend on the size of the clock itself. If we assume that the clock has a diameter of 8.5 inches (since the circumference is given), then we can calculate the area it occupies on the wall as follows:

Find the radius of the clock:

Circumference = 2πr

8.5 = 2πr

r = 8.5 / (2π) ≈ 1.35 inches

Find the area of the circle:

Area = πr²

Area = π(1.35)² ≈ 5.71 square inches

Therefore, the circular wall clock with a circumference of 8.5 inches and a diameter of approximately 1.35 inches occupies an area of approximately 5.71 square inches on the wall.
