24. Rewrite the following sentences, placing the phrase and clause modifiers in the parenthesis in correct position. The people enjoyed the concert. (who came)


The clause modifier in the given is a relative clause.

A relative clause is used as an adjective and always appears after the noun it modifies.

Relative pronouns include who, whom, whose, where, when, that and which.

Since a relative clause appears after the noun it modifies the sentence should be:

The people who came enjoyed the concert.
