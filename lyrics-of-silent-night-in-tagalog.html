Answer:

Silent Night is an example of the Strophic Form Binary Form Binary form is a musical form in two related sections, both of which are usually repeated.

Silent night is an example of Strophic along with Amazing grace and While shepherds watch their flocks at night.

What is a Strophic from?

Strophic is a type of song (usually folk and hymns) that uses the same melody for each verse with various or different text for each repetition.

How to tell if a song is an example of Strophic?

The melody is repeated for each of the verses but the lyrics are different every verse.
