24. what are the types of hedging?


1. Introductory Verbs (e.g. look like, think, believe, appear to be, tend, seem, indicate)

2. Lexical Verbs (e.g. assume, believe, suggest)

3. Modal Verbs (e.g. must, will, could, might, may, would)

4. Adverbs of Frequency (e.g. sometimes, often, usually)

5. Modal Adverbs (e.g. clearly, definitely, possibly, certainly, perhaps)

6. Modal Adjectives (e.g. clear, possible, definite, probably, certain)

7. Modal Nouns (e.g. possibility, assumption, probability)

8. That Clauses (e.g. There is every hope that, It might be suggested that, It could be noted that)

9. To clause + noun (e.g. It is important to develop, It may possible to obtain)
