Answer:

Castiel, often shortened to Cass, is a powerful angel of the Lord who was responsible for saving Dean Winchester from Hell, on the archangels' command. It was his job to help prevent Lilith from breaking the 66 Seals that, once broken, would release Lucifer from his cage in Hell.

Answer:

1. His father is 34 years old.

2. Cyanne's mother weight is 55 kg.

Step-by-step explanation:

Castiel is 8 years old. His father is 10 years more than thrice his age. How old is his father?

Transform in equation

Castiel father's = 10 +3 (8)

= 10 + 24

= 34 yrs old

Cyanne weighs 20 kilograms. What is her mother's weight if she is 15 kilograms more than twice Cyanne's weight?

Form an equation

Cyanne's mother weight = 15 + 2( 20)

= 15 + 40 = 55 kg
