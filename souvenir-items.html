3. The city government spent ₱10000 producing souvenir items for the Kadayawan and will sell the souvenir items for ₱450 each. If the city government sold 75 souvenir items, how much money did city government make?


Answer:

Php. 23, 750

Step-by-step explanation:

In mathematics, solving word problem required knowledge and skills in basic mathematics that will improve your analytical and critical thinking skills.

In the given problem, you need to apply your knowledge and skills in alegbra and basic math.

GivenPhp. 10, 000 spent moneyPhp. 450 worth of souvenir75 items of souvenir was sold

ProblemTotal money make by the government (profit)

Solution

Total Gain = Number of items × Price of each item

Profit = Total Gain - Total Cost

Profit = 450(75) - 10, 000

Profit = 33, 750 - 10, 000

Profit = 23, 750

Therefore, the money make (total profit earned) is Php. 23, 750.

29. Some souvenir items are distributed among some participants in a conference. If each receives 10, there are 3 items left. If each receives 11, there are 4 items too few. How many souvenir items are there? ​


Answer:

Therefore, there least number of souvenir items is 73.

Step-by-step explanation:

Start with when the number is divided by 11, there are 4 too few.

It means that only 7 items are received by the last recipient lacking 4 items.

List the multiples of 11:

11 ⇒  11, 22, 33, 44, 55, 66, 77, 88, 99, 110

Pick a multiple of 11 that when 4 is subtracted, a multiple of ten has a remainder 3.  That number is 77.

77 - 4 = 73

Conditions:

If each receives 10, there are 3 items left:

73 ÷ 10 = 7 remainder 3

7 participants receive 10 items each with 3 items left or remainder.

If each receives 11, there are 4 too few:

73 ÷ 11 = 6 remainder 7

6 participants receive 11 souvenir items, while 1 participant receives only 7 items which is 4 too few or lacking 4 items to become 11.

Therefore, there least number of souvenir items is 73.
