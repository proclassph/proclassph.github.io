Answer:

Ang Batas Underwood-Simmons o mas kilala rin bilang Revenue Act of 1913 ng Estados Unidos ay ang pagsasabalik ng pederal na buwis at pagbababa ng taripa mula 40% tungo 25%, na naunang naipataw sa pamamagitan ng Payne-Aldrich Tariff Act ng 1909.

What is the theme of the story in jake by Jim bartlett?

Personalized Narrative Theme

Because the whole book concentrates around the character's real-world experience or a third-person narration of his work as a police officer, I'd classify it as a personalized narrative theme. It depicts how Jim, the protagonist, conducts his everyday life, thoughts, preparations, and activities while he commutes to work. As stated in the text, he was touched by his encounter with these current concerns in our society. Moreover, the present world events have given me empathy, or the ability to understand and share his feelings. These kinds of things still happen today, and in some cases, they're getting worse.

What is the point of view of the story in jake by jim bartlett?

The point-of-view used in the flash fiction is Third Person View, because it uses the pronouns of He, She, They, and narrates the character's life.

12. ano ang underwood-simmonous act


The Underwood-Simmons Act was supported by President Woodrow Wilson, who believed the act would lead to an increase in the import of foreign materials and reduce their cost. The Underwood-Simmons act also introduced the first income tax in the United States as an attempt to recover the revenue that had been lost by lowering the tariffs on foreign imports.
