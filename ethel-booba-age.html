12. The Witches coven are making a potion and Ethel, Glinda, Locasta and Circe each add a different ingredient. When the potion is finished, there is 1/4 of the bat blood left, 1/3 of the knotgrass, 1/2 of the moonstone and 5/8 of the unicorn hair. Ethel used the most. Glinda used the least. Locasta used more than Circe. Who used the moonstone?


Answer:

Circe

Step-by-step explanation:

Convert the fractions ingredients left to similar fractions.

Bat blood: [tex]\frac{1}{4}=\frac{6}{24}[/tex]

Knotgrass: [tex]\frac{1}{3}=\frac{8}{24}[/tex]

Moonstone: [tex]\frac{1}{2}=\frac{12}{24}[/tex]

Unicorn hair: [tex]\frac{5}{8}=\frac{15}{24}[/tex]

By analogy, the more part of ingredient left, the lesser it is used, and vice versa. Since Ethel used the most while Glinda used the least ingredient, Ethel used bat blood while Glinda used unicorn hair. Moreover, since Locasta used more than Circe, Locasta used knotgrass while Circe used the moonstone.

Locasta used more than Circe. Who used the moonstone?


The moonstone was used by Circe. In making the potion, Ethel used the most, next is Locasta, then Circe, and finally Glinda who used the least. The bat blood ([tex]\frac{1}{4}[/tex] left) was used the most, next is knotgrass ([tex]\frac{1}{3}[/tex] left), then moonstone ([tex]\frac{1}{2}[/tex] left), and unicorn hair ([tex]\frac{5}{8}[/tex] left) which was used the least.

The Witches coven made a potion in which Ethel, Glinda, Locasta and Circe each add a different ingredient. Below, the witches are arranged in order of who used the most ingredient to the least:  

Ethel Locasta Circe Glinda

The following describes each ingredient in terms of the fraction of the amount left (and used) together with their decimal equivalent:

Bat blood - [tex]\frac{1}{4}[/tex] or 0.25 was left of this ingredient meaning [tex]\frac{3}{4}[/tex] or 0.75 was used.

Knotgrass – [tex]\frac{1}{3}[/tex] or 0.33 was left which means [tex]\frac{2}{3}[/tex] or 0.67 of this ingredient was used.

Moonstone – [tex]\frac{1}{2}[/tex] or 0.5 was left meaning [tex]\frac{1}{2}[/tex] or 0.5 was used.

Unicorn hair – [tex]\frac{5}{8}[/tex] or 0.625 was left which means [tex]\frac{3}{8}[/tex] or 0.375 was used.

Below, the ingredients are arranged in order of which was used the most to the least:

Bat blood Knotgrass Moonstone Unicorn hair

 

Moonstone ranked 3rd in the order of which ingredient was used the most. Consequently, the 3rd in the order of who used the most ingredient is Circe. This means that Circe used the moonstone.
