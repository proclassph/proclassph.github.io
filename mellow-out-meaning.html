Assonance definition

Assonance is repetition of vowel sounds in adjacent words usually occurs in the same line. This sound is usually more common in pressurized than unstressed sounds.

Definition of poetic device

What is meant by a poetic device is an element that is contained in and composes a text that is used to obtain aspects of the beauty of language and the delivery of meaning. This poetic device covers aspects of the use of words which include sound, meaning, arrangement in the poetry text.

Another explanation, poetic device is a technique or device used in poetry that helps increase the beauty of the form, image, and depth of meaning of a poem. Poets only rely on words as a tool to express their ideas. Therefore, the use of these words must be considered properly so that they can contain beautiful sounds, unexpected meanings, sequences that allow the reader to understand them, and have depth of thought, emotion and empathy that can bring up an image. So that the reader really feels to see, hear and feel what is conveyed by the author.
