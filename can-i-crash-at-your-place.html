18. can you crash atom?​


Answer:

Can you crush atoms? Not normally, but there are certain types of stars, called neutron stars, that are made from 'crushed' atoms. The gravitational field is high enough in a neutron star that the electrons around an atom are forced to combine with the protons in the nucleus, creating neutrons


19. Can a driver who had the opportunity to avoid a road crash and neglected to avoid such road crash be jointly held liable?


Yes, the driver who had the opportunity to evade could be held liable but ultimate responsibility rests with anyone who violates the rules and causes an accident.

Explanation :

Immediately after an accident, you need to decide who is responsible. The guilty person is the one who caused the accident. In some situations there is more than one guilty person. However, the person responsible for paying for the damage may be someone else who was not involved in the accident, for example the employer or the company.

As is usually stated in the doctrine, a person who had a clear last chance or opportunity to avoid an accident, regardless of the negligent act of his opponent or the alleged act of a third person against him, is held liable by law, for the consequences of the accident.
