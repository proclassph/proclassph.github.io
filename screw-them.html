23. screw is usescrew is used for ​


Answer:

Screws are one kind of simple machines. They have a corkscrew-shaped ridge, known as a thread, wrapped around a cylinder. The head is specially shaped to allow a screwdriver or wrench to grip the screw when driving it in.

The most common uses of screws are to hold objects together — such as wood — and to position objects. Often screws have a head on one end of the screw that allows it to be turned. The head is usually larger than the body of the screw. The cylindrical portion of the screw from the underside of the head to the tip is called the shank.

Bolts are a type of screw that usually is designed to work with a nut or another threaded fastener.

With one hand, hold the screwdriver's tip while turning the handle with the other. Grasp the handle with your dominant hand and the tip with your other hand as close to the screw head as possible. Turn the screwdriver clockwise to tighten the screw and counterclockwise to loosen it with necessary force.
