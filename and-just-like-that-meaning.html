ANSWER:

This isn’t actually a simile. The use of the word “like” doesn’t automatically make something a simile. It also has to be a comparison, which this is not. In this case, “like” is a conjunction joining the verb shuffled and the thing that is shuffled (a deck of cards).

16. What does it mean when you feel like you don't need anyone help, you just want to do yourself? like example: “you trying to calmdown by yourself” but you don't need nor wanted to anyone helping you, you just want to yourselves? What caused?​


Answer:

Feeling like you don't need anyone's help and wanting to do things on your own can stem from a variety of reasons. Here are some possibilities:

Independence: You may have a strong desire for independence and prefer to handle things on your own without relying on others. This could be due to personal beliefs or past experiences that have made you feel more self-sufficient.

Control: You may feel more in control of the situation when you handle it on your own. It's possible that you don't want to rely on others because you feel like they may not handle the situation the way you want them to.

Trust: It's possible that you don't trust others to help you. You may feel like they don't have your best interests in mind or that they may not be capable of helping you the way you need them to.

Pride: Sometimes, we may feel like we don't want to burden others with our problems or appear weak by asking for help. This could be due to pride or a desire to maintain a certain image in front of others.

Coping mechanisms: Sometimes, people prefer to cope with stress or difficult situations on their own as a way of managing their emotions. This could be due to past experiences where seeking help did not provide the desired outcome, or a preference for solitary activities as a means of self-care.

It's important to note that while it's okay to want to handle things on your own, it's also important to recognize when you may need help and be open to accepting it. Seeking support from others can be a healthy way of coping and can provide a different perspective on the situation at hand.
