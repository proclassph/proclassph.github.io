11. "velocity"1. joo walked 210m east to his friend's house in 105seconds.What will be average velocity?​


Answer:

To find the average velocity, we need to divide the displacement (change in position) by the time taken.

In this case, Joo walked 210m to the east, so his displacement is +210m (positive because he walked east). The time taken was 105 seconds.

Therefore, the average velocity is:

average velocity = displacement / time taken

average velocity = +210m / 105s

average velocity = 2 m/s east

So Joo's average velocity was 2 m/s to the east.
