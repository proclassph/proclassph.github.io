Answer: Yan po example sana makatulong

A biased statement is a statement that is based on a personal preference or a personal interest.

First example “These sandwiches are horrible’ is a biased statement made by someone who doesn’t like the taste of one or more of the ingredients on the sandwich. It is a personal opinion, not an objective statement based on neutral evidence.

Second example “Sarah is the best person for the job” is a biased statement if Sarah is the daughter of the person who says it. This opinion may or may not be true and needs to be backed up by other evidence as well from sources other than the opinion of someone who has a personal interest.
