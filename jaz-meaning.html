1. m is in the interior of angle jaz, angle jam=40 degrees, angle maz=30 degrees. find angle jaz​


Answer:

∠JAZ = 70°

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

M is in the interior of ∠JAZTherefore, ∠JAZ is divided into ∠JAM and ∠MAZ;∠JAM = 40°∠MAZ = 30°

By addition postulate:

∠JAM + ∠MAZ = ∠JAZ

∠JAZ = 40° + 30°

∠JAZ = 70°
