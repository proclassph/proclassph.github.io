Low-carbohydrate:

diets restrict carbohydrate consumption relative to the average diet. Foods high in carbohydrates (e.g., sugar, bread, pasta) are limited, and replaced with foods containing a higher percentage of fat and protein (e.g., meat, poultry, fish, shellfish, eggs, cheese, nuts, and seeds), as well as low carbohydrate foods . spinach, kale, chard, collards, and other fibrous vegetablesLow-Carb, High-Fat:

stands for “low-carb, high-fat.” It’s a fairly standard very-low-carb diet but with an even greater emphasis on whole, unprocessed foods.

It focuses mostly on meats, fish and shellfish, eggs, healthy fats, vegetables, dairy products, nuts, and berries.

The recommended carb intake on this diet can range from 20–100 grams per day.

Low-Carb Paleo Diet;The paleo diet is currently one of the world’s most popular ways of eating. It encourages eating foods that were likely available in the Paleolithic era before the agricultural and industrial revolutions.

According to paleo proponents, returning to the diet of your prehistoric ancestors should improve health because humans allegedly evolved and adapted to eating such foods.

Several small studies show that a paleo diet can cause weight loss, reduce blood sugars, and improve risk factors for heart disease (13Trusted Source, 14Trusted Source, 15Trusted Source).

Importance of Meal Planning

There are a lot of reasons why meal planning is important. Three factors emphasizing the importance of meal planning include budget planning, nutritional management, and maintenance of desired diet. The importance of meal planning with regards to budget is that when an individual or a group has a strict or a goal to spend money according to their means, they will be able to do so and plan ahead on how they will use their food budget. Moreover, the importance of meal planning also considers the nutrition or desired nutrients to gain from the meals being planned ahead. Similarly, maintaining a balanced diet or preferred weight is also regarded.

Further Importance of Meal Planning

Meal planning is also important because of the following reasons:

It helps an individual or group to have a varied menu for days, weeks, or even a month.It also saves time by planning out the items to be bought and the schedule to buy them versus going back and forth to the grocery store to buy missed out ingredients.It ensures the preparation of healthy and delicious food.It greatly considers the diet of an individual or each person in a group when it comes to their needs, food restrictions, etc.It reduces the generation of waste.

24. Is it okay for the Athletes to adhere to a low-Carb Diet?__________________________________________________________________________________________________________.​


Answer:

Low-carb or ketogenic diets can be a good choice for healthy people who are mostly exercising and lifting to stay healthy. However, there is currently no solid evidence that they improve performance over higher-carb diets in athletes.

As an athlete, if you're in good health and your body weight is normal, you do not need to cut carbs. Because carbohydrates are the body's main source of energy, cutting them out of your diet can cause serious fatigue and performance drops. Carbohydrates also work with protein to help build muscle.
