8. Rieza walks faster than Naya with the speed of about 0.65 m/s. The canteen is 15 meters away from their classroom. How many seconds will it take her to go to the canteen?


CALCULATING TIME

Problem:

Rieza walks faster than Naya with the speed of about 0.65 m/s. The canteen is 15 meters away from their classroom. How many seconds will it take her to go to the canteen?

Given:

Speed of 0.65 m/s and distance of 15 meters.

To find:

How many seconds will it take for Rieza to go to the canteen?

Solution:

Time = distance ÷ speed

Solving:

15 ÷ 0.65 = 23. 07

FINAL ANSWER: It will takes 23.07 for Rieza to go to the canteen.

27. can anyone give an explanation or short analysis of casuality by naya valdellon


Answer:

"Casuality" by Naya Valdellon is a short story that explores the theme of fate and the consequences of our actions. The story follows a woman named Mia who is attending her friend's wedding in the Philippines. During the reception, Mia meets a man named Arnie and feels an immediate connection with him. As the night progresses, they spend more time together and eventually end up sleeping together. The next morning, Arnie reveals that he is engaged and regrets what happened between them.

The story examines the idea of causality and how one action can have a significant impact on our lives. Mia's decision to sleep with Arnie has consequences that ripple throughout the rest of the story. The author also explores the themes of regret and the idea that we can never truly know what the future holds.

Overall, "Casuality" is a thought-provoking story that raises important questions about the choices we make and the consequences that follow. It is a reminder that our actions have real-world consequences and that we should be mindful of the impact they can have on our lives and the lives of those around us.
