23. how did karl calculate the sum of counting numbers from 1 to 100 in just a very short span of time?​


Answer:

Carl Friedrich Gauss was able to find the sum of the set of counting numbers: 1+2+3+4+5+...+99+100 within seconds only because he realized that the sum of each paired numbers such as 1+100, 2+99, 3+98, 4+97,... are all equal to 101. He then just multiplied 101 by 50 since there are 50 such pairs so that the product is simply 5050.
