18. J Click to add title Game of Chance 2. (A roulette wheel) A roulette wheel in a fiesta carnival has the numbers 1 through 30. If you bet P5.00 you will have a chance to win a prepaid load worth P100.00. Find the expectation if you play a bet. 2023/03/23 08:01 ​


To find the expectation of playing the bet, we need to calculate the probability of winning and the expected value of winning.

The probability of winning can be calculated as 1/30, since there is only one winning number out of a total of 30 numbers.

The expected value of winning is the product of the probability of winning and the amount won, minus the cost of playing the bet. In this case, the amount won is P100.00 and the cost of playing the bet is P5.00.

So the expectation can be calculated as follows:

Expectation = (1/30) x P100.00 - P5.00

Expectation = P3.33

Therefore, the expectation of playing the bet is P3.33. This means that on average, for every P5.00 bet, you can expect to win back P3.33.
