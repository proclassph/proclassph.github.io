7. is a plant belonging to a fern family that grows abundantly in the hinterlands of mindanao


Answer:

Nito Vines

Explanation:

Nito Vines (Lygodium circinatum) is a plant belonging to the fern family that grows abundantly in the hinterlands of Mindanao in Southern Philippines.

It is a vine growing as a secondary forest cover clinging to trees and rocks. This climbing fern is very common in the Philippines at low and medium altitudes.

How do you harvest Nito?

The nito vine must be gathered first then exposed to sunlight to dry. It does not need treatment like soaking, scraping or hacking processes to enhance its natural beauty and color. The strip is taken from the innermost core of the stem, which is about the diameter of a pencil.

baskets placemats plate chargerscoastersbox containers
