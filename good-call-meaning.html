1. The marketing of goods or services by means of telephone calls, typically unsolicited, to potential customers?


This is called B2B Telemarketing.  B2B is short for business to business, and since the medium of communication is through unsolicited telephone calls, the word telemarketing is used.

The principle is to promote the business through contacting customers via telephone.  Once the customer likes the product, the basic transaction begins.  This is a new, shortcut way of selling goods and products instead of a traditional door to door and market to market approach.
