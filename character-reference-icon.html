21. Which among the given factors (referring to icons presented above) has the most important part in the development of a person? Why? ​


The  factors have the most important part in the development of a person:

1. Heredity (congenital or hereditary)

2.Environmental conditions

3.The role of maturity

4.Social environment

5.Social Status

6.Culture, race and ethnicity

7. Historical context

EXPLANATION:

1. Heredity (congenital)

Here, the dominant factor is in terms of biology, such as hair color, skin color, etc., but behavior also affects the attitude of the child.

2.Environmental conditions

Environmental conditions are one of the most important factors in human development, because the environment is the first nature he touches, especially the family environment. But what is meant here is not that, but the times that need to be considered by parents, including:

Prenatal: maternal health, maternal nutrition, use of chemicals (in the use of drugs), and the mother's emotional state.Christmas: type of birth (normal, breech, caesarean section), maternal treatment. In this case, usually the IQ of a child born normally is higher than that of a child who was born breech and had surgery.Post natal: gender, nutritional age, care, sensitivity to disease, chronic disease, hormones, parental attitudes and stimulation.

3.The role of maturity

Maturity in terms of operational maturity can be defined as the level of psychological functioning that a person can achieve, after which the level of psychological functioning no longer increases with age.

4.Social environment

It has been explained above that the environment is one of the factors that greatly influence human development, because the environment is an important factor for the growth and development of the child, from the environment he socializes, can talk and interact.

5.Social Status

Social status is one of the factors that shape the attitudes and behavior of children, usually children whose parents are of high social status, usually they think more quickly than children who live with parents who are just ordinary people.

6.Culture, race and ethnicity

Actually, racial and ethnic culture has been included in the social environment, because culture, race and ethnicity exist in the social environment.

7. Historical context

The historical context here, is an event that he has experienced, which is able to inspire or change the attitude of the child, it can have a negative impact, it can also have a positive impact.
