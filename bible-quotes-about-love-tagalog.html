18. Bible verse about love of god for people


Answer:

Best bible verse about love of God for people

For me, the best bible verse about love of God for people is John 3:16 "For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life."

Why?

Because of His love for us, He saved us and gave His only begotten son for us to believe and to be redeemed. He wants us to give blessings everyday. He gave us new life and new beginning and by His grace and mercy, we are blessed. Because of this, we are His children and He is our father. Our father who never stops to love us, to forgive us, to gives us blessings. He is our provider and protector. His love covers us from any harm and danger. The kind of love that He gives us everyday is called unconditional love.

Other Bible Verses that we can read:John 15:13 - "Greater love has no one than this: to lay down one’s life for one’s friends."Psalm 86:15 - "But you, Lord, are a compassionate and gracious God, slow to anger, abounding in love and faithfulness."Romans 5:8 - "But God demonstrates his own love for us in this: While we were still sinners, Christ died for us."Deuteronomy 7:9 - "Know therefore that the LORD your God is God; he is the faithful God, keeping his covenant of love to a thousand generations of those who love him and keep his commandments."Ephesians 2:4-5 - "But because of his great love for us, God, who is rich in mercy, 5 made us alive with Christ even when we were dead in transgressions—it is by grace you have been saved."

Why do we need to reflect on those bible verses?

For us to be encouraged and reminded how wide and how deep is the love of God for His people. Just like He did to Israelite and other people who called and believe in Him. He always rescue us and protects us.

Because of His love we are here. And because of His love we are restored.

Let us be reminded to do our part and give thanks to Him every single day. Because without God, we are nothing.
