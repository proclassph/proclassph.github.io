3. What is hague i and ii during world war one?


ww1

Along with the Geneva Conventions, the Hague Conventions were among the first formal statements of the laws of war and war crimes in the body of secular international law. A third conference was planned for 1914 and later rescheduled for 1915, but it did not take place because of the start of World War I.

ww2

In the early morning of 10 May 1940, Germany invaded the Netherlands. The Hague became the centre of German rule in the Netherlands.

14. Please explain :There is also an increased role in international organizations like the United Nations and the international Criminal Court in Hague, the role of non-governmental organizations like Doctors Without Borders or Amnesty International, and the role of global media.


Answer:

(see explanation)

Explanation:

As countries continue to progress and improve, there will come a point that they need to interact with other countries. Sometimes one country has a shortage of resources and another country has a lot. They can negotiate and get deals from each other to ensure that both of them continue to develop. However, some of these negotiations might gain traction from countries both involved and not involved. In this case, there should exist a mediating party to avoid conflict escalation, impose the rule of law and keep peace. That is one of the purposes of the UN and the ICC. These are headed by member countries that will subject themselves under the authority of the laws agreed upon here. Each country has representatives to ensure fair trial and that little bias will be made.

The ICC and UN act as the court for country disputes like how a regular court resolves conflicts on the people involved.

NGOs that promote goodwill also exist because these NGOs believe that some countries are at a shortage of whatever good or product they offer. For example, doctors without borders go to countries where they believe medical care is not efficiently provided. They also exist as an international body because bureaucracy, or the interaction between countries often delays processes. They want to bypass this, and offer good health to everyone. Other countries also allow this as this is not an act that another country provides but by another entity. This means that they will not deal with another country's government and just an organization instead. Other services that involve this are aid requests that happen during disasters or international media that some countries use to inform the world and give them an overview of what is happening on their countries.

22. Ano ang dahilan bakit hindi naging mabisa ang Hague Court ofArbitration?​pls I need the answer right now


Answer:

Dahil hindi obligado ang isang bansang mapailalim dito.

Explanation:

Ang Hague Court of Arbitration na itinatag noong 1899 ay hindi naging mabisa dahil hindi naman obligado ang isang bansang mapailalim dito. Ang unang pagpupulong sa Hague noong 1899 na pinatnubayan ni Czar Nicholas II ng Rusya ay nagtatag sa samahan ngunit nabigo rin sa pagbabawas ng mga armas. Ang pangalawang pagpupulong sa Hague ay noong 1907, sa mungkahi ni Pangulong 12 Theodore Roosevelt. Nabigo rin ito sa layuning magpabawas ng armas ngunit nagkaroon ng unawaan tungkol sa lalong makataong paglalabanan. Sa kasamaang-palad, ang mga kasunduang ito ay nabura nang sumiklab ang Unang Digmaang Pandaigdig.
