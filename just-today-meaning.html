Step-by-step explanation:

μ = mean = 1,000

σ = standard deviation = 100

x = 1,100

[tex]z = \frac{x - μ}{σ} \\ \\ \: \: \: \: = \frac{1100 - 1000}{100} \\ \\ \: \: \: \: = \frac{100}{100} \\ \\ \: \: \: \: = 1 \\ \\ [/tex]
