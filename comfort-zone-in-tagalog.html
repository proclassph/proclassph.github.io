11. In your owb understanding what is comfort zone?​


In my understanding, a comfort zone refers to a state of mind where an individual feels safe, secure, and in control of their environment. It is a place or situation where a person feels comfortable and familiar, and where they do not experience anxiety or stress. Comfort zones can be physical, psychological, or emotional, and can be different for each individual. For example, a person may feel comfortable in their own home, in a familiar routine, or in a certain type of social situation. While comfort zones can provide a sense of security and stability, they can also limit personal growth and prevent individuals from trying new things or taking on new challenges.
