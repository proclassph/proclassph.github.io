Ang Mesenteric adenitis ay tumutukoy sa isang karamdaman na nakakaapekto sa mga lymph node sa rehiyon ng tiyan. Ang nangungunang sanhi ay karaniwang impeksyon, kahit na ang iba pang mga bihirang mga sanhi ay posible rin, tulad ng kanser o mga kondisyon sa bakterya. Ang Mesenteric adenitis ay sanhi ng pamamaga ng mga lymph node. Karamihan sa mga apektadong indibidwal ay mga tinedyer at bata. Ang mga sintomas ay may posibilidad na malinis sa kanilang sarili. Kung kinakailangan, magagamit ang mga antibiotics at iba pang paggamot, kabilang ang operasyon. Alamin ang mga sintomas, sanhi, at paggamot para sa mesenteric adenitis.

Sakit sa tiyan

Ang pinakasikat na sintomas ng mesenteric adenitis ay sakit ng tiyan. Dahil sa kondisyong ito ang sanhi ng pamamaga ng mga lymph node sa tiyan, ang sakit ay madalas na nadama sa paligid ng lugar na ito. Kahit na ang eksaktong lokasyon ng kakulangan sa ginhawa ay mahirap na tukuyin, ang mas mababang kanang bahagi ay madalas na ang pinaka-mahina na rehiyon. Ang ilang mga tao ay nakakaranas ng sakit sa isang partikular na bahagi lamang ng tiyan, samantalang ang iba ay nakadarama ng sakit sa buong tiyan. Dapat mong tawagan ang iyong doktor sa lalong madaling panahon kung napansin mo ang matinding sakit bigla, o sakit na sinamahan ng lagnat.

The encaustic painting, also known as hot wax painting, uses the heated beeswax to which colored pigments are added, and the results are some of the most interesting and elaborate abstract paintings but more famously the known Fayum mummy portraits from Egypt (produced around 100 – 300 AD), and the icons of the Greek Art, dating as far back as the 5th-century.

Wax is an excellent preserver of materials and it was from this application that the style of encaustic painting developed. Special metal instruments and brushes are employed to shape the liquid or paste before it cools down, and today, the process of melting the wax is made easier with the help of heat lamps, heat guns, irons, and other methods of applying the heat onto the canvas, or prepared wooden surfaces. 17. Rob weighs 120 kilograms, he is 3 times heavier than Aiden. What is the weight of Aiden? ​


LINEAR EQUATIONS

[tex]__________________________[/tex]

Given:Rob's weight = 120 kgAiden's weight = xAnswer & Solution:

As what stated in the problem, Aiden's weight is three times heavier than Aiden so it will be represented as 3x. We can turn this into a linear equation to solve for Aiden's weight.

[tex]\sf 3x = 120[/tex]

Divide 3 on both sides of the equation.

[tex]\sf \frac{3x}{3} = \frac{120}{3} [/tex]

[tex]\sf x = \boxed{ \sf 40}[/tex]

∴ Aiden's weight is 40 kilograms.

28. what is the meaning of amen


well it means so be it in the prayers



We say Amen many times, after we pray or if we receive communion.

The word amen comes from the Hebrew word āmēn, which means "certainty" or "Truth".

We say it after prayers because we agree to what we said in our prayers. For example, we'll pray the "Hail Mary"


Hail Mary full of Grace, the Lord is with thee.

Blessed are thou among women and blessed is the fruit of thy womb Jesus.

Holy Mary Mother of God,

pray for us sinners now and at the hour of our death

Amen.


We say amen because we agree that Mary is full of grace. We say Amen because we agree that Mary is the mother of God.

In communion, we receive the body of Christ. the priest will say "The body of Christ" and our response is: "Amen". We say amen because indeed This is the body of Christ. We say amen because we agree that Christ is present in that bread.
