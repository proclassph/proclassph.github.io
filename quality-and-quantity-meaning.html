17. write true if the statement is correct and false if it is not correct15.two ratios are proportion is the product of the equal to the product of the extremes16.indirect proportion when one quantity increases the other quantity decreases of the same rate and vice versa17.in partitive proportion a whole is divided into parts that is proportional to the given ratio18.inverse proportion when one quality decreased another equal to increased and vice versa19.in ratio and proportion the first and the last term are called means while the second and third term are called extreme math​


Answer:

Answer:

15. True

A proportion is simply a statement that two ratios are equal. It can be written in two ways: as two equal fractions a/b = c/d; or using a colon, a:b = c:d. We can also use cross products to find a missing term in a proportion.

16. False

Direct proportion is the relationship between two variables whose ratio is equal to a constant value. In other words, direct proportion is a situation where an increase in one quantity causes a corresponding increase in the other quantity, or a decrease in one quantity results in a decrease in the other quantity.

17. True

A partitive proportion, or proportion by parts, describes a whole amount being divided or distributed into two or more unequal parts. Example: The ratio of boys to girls in a class is 3:5.

18. True

Inversely Proportional

If one variable decreases, the other decreases in the same proportion. Inversely proportional variables are those in which one variable decreases with the increase in another variable and one variable increases with the decrease in another variable. It is opposite to direct proportion.

19. False

We know, the first term (1st) and the fourth term (4th) of a proportion are called extreme terms or extremes, and the second term (2nd) and the third term (3rd) are called middle terms or means. Therefore, in a proportion, product of extremes = product of middle terms.
