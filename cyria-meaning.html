4. A chandelier costs 8,250. Ms. Syria has only 6,215. How much more money does Ms. Syria need?​


Answer:

A chandelier costs 8,250. Ms. Syria has only 6,215. How much more money does Ms. Syria need?

Ms. Syria needs 2,035 to buy the chandelier.

Step-by-step explanation:

What is asked?

How much more money does Ms. Syria need?

Given:

cost of chandelier - 8,250

Ms. Syria's money - 6,215

Solution:

To know how much more money Ms. Syria needs to buy the chandelier, we need to take away the amount that she has from the cost of the chandelier.

(cost of chandelier - Ms. Syria's money = amount that Ms. Syria needs)

8,250 - 6,215 = 2,035

Let us check if our answer is correct.

6,215 + 2,035 = 8,250

8,250 = 8,250 ✔

Ito ay binubuo ng mga bulubundukin sa kanluran at steppe na tumutukoy sa malawak na madamong kapatagan. Higit din sa kalahati ng bansang ito ay nababalot ng disyerto.

Sila ay nakararanas ng Mediterranean na klima. Tuwing tag-init ay tuyot at tuwing taglamig naman ay maaring maging katamtaman ang panahon o mas malamig pa.

Tinatayang nasa 85% ng kanilang populasyon ay mga Muslim. Malaking bahagdan ng populasyon sa Syria ang nabubuhay sa tulong ng industriya ng agrikultura dahil sa kanilang topograpiya. Katulad ng iba pang mga Arab Countries, ang bansang Syria ay mayaman sa oil.
