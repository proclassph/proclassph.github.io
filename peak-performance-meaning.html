13. It means a person has reached the peak of his/ her potential?a.safetyb.self-steemc.self-actualizationd.physichological​


C. Self-Actualization

Further explanation:

Self-Actualization

When a person has reached the peak of his potential, it is called self-actualization.

Self-actualization is a need for an individual to use, develop and then utilize the potential, talents and capacities possessed to then produce and manifest himself according to his wishes.

Indicators of Self-Actualization Needs

According to Robbins and Coulter (2010: 110), indicators of self-actualization needs include the following:

The need for growth (growth need), this is a need to know and understand something, to grow and also to develop with the respect of others.

The need for achieving one's potential, this is a person's need to develop the potential, abilities, and talents that exist within him to the fullest.

Self-fulfillment needs (self-fulfillment), this is a need in fulfilling one's existence by maximizing the use of abilities and also the potential contained in him.

The need for encouragement, this is an impulse from within the individual to then be able to maintain his existence in accordance with the potential he has.

Explanation of other answers:

A. Safety

Safety is a state of security, in a safe condition physically, socially, spiritually, financially, politically, emotionally, occupationally, psychologically, or educationally and protected from threats.

B. Self-esteem

Self-esteem is an evaluation process aimed at the individual at himself, which later relates to the process of individual acceptance of himself. In this case the evaluation will describe how the individual evaluates himself, shows appreciation and recognition or not, and shows the extent to which the individual feels capable, successful and valuable.

D. Psychological

Psychological is a factor that comes from within a person's individual and these psychological elements include motivation, perception, learning, personality, memory, emotions, beliefs, and attitudes. Psychological is something that is personality and individual ability to use it to deal with stress caused by situation and environment.

Answer:

The peak at the mean indicates that the highest concentration of data points occurs at the mean. This is also sometimes referred to as the "mode" of the data set.
