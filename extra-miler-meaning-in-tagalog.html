10. The mean is 15 numbers is 12. An extra number is included and the mean increases to 13. What is the extra number?


Given:

Mean of 15 numbers is 12.

An extra number is added.

The mean is increased to 13.

To Find:

The extra number

Solution

Find the total of the 15 numbers:

Mean = 12

Total = 15 x 12

Total = 180

Find the total of the 16 numbers:

Man = 13

Total = 16 x 13

Total = 208

Find the extra number:

Extra number = 208 - 180

Extra number = 28

Answer: The extra number is 28
