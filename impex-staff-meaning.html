Always be specific.

There are 2 possible answers for that question.

1) 3 staff members

2) 16 staff members

1) If some of the teachers staff are busy at teaching their students, only 3 members (teachers) can attend the meeting. 13/16 =

13 members - 16 members = 3 members.

2) but If the question was meant to be "How many staff members are (still) invited to the meeting?" the answer is still 16 members. Because the total invited members from "13/16" is still a 16 members.

7. what is the meaning of staff in music​


Answer:

staff, also spelled stave, in the notation of Western music, five parallel horizontal lines that, with a clef, indicate the pitch of musical notes. ... A precise staff notation made possible composition of the complex polyphonic works that characterize Western art music.

what is the meaning of staff in music?

Answer:

The lines and spaces are numbered from bottom to top; the bottom line is the first line and the top line is the fifth line. The musical staff is analogous to a mathematical graph of pitch with respect to time. Pitches of notes are given by their vertical position on the staff and notes are played from left to right.
