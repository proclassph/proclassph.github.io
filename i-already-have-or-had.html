2. I can't believe it. Father ____ for my birthday.

A. Had already prepared

B. Will have been preparing

C. Is preparing

D. Has prepared

Option D. is the correct one.

Why?

❌ HAD ALREADY PREPARED is Past Perfect Tense.❌ WILL HAVE BEEN PREPARING is Future Perfect Progressive Tense.❌ IS PREPARING is Present Progressive Tense.✔️ HAS PREPARED is Present Perfect Tense.

The given sentence said that the aspect is a Present Perfect Tense.

The formula for Present Perfect Tense is: have/has + the past participle of the verb.

Examples:

have + [past participle of cook] = have cooked

I have cooked adobo for my breakfast.

has + [past participle of choose] = has chosen

She has chosen the brainliest answer.
