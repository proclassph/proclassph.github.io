3. why the critical line of the complex plane, where the non-trivial zeros of the Riemann zeta function are thought to lie, is significant.


Answer:

The critical line is significant because it is where the Riemann hypothesis asserts that all nontrivial (complex) Riemann zeta function zeros lie. The first few zeros of the Riemann zeta function occur on this line.

15. The Gamma Zeta Beta fraternity must choose a committee of four members to plan its annual Children's Hospital fund raiser and beer bash. If the fraternity has 10 members, how many different committees can be chosen?


PROBLEM SOLVING

Given

Members in the fratenity = 10Required members in the committee = 4Different committees can be chosen = ?

You can use the formula of permutations to deteremine the number of ways to form a committee.

P (n,r) = n! / (n - r)!

P (10,6) = 10! / (10 - 6)!

P (10,6) = 10! / 4!

P (10,6) = 10 • 9 • 8 • 7 • 6 • 5 • 4! / 4!

P (10,6) = 10 • 9 • 8 • 7 • 6 • 5

P (10,6) = 151,200 committees
