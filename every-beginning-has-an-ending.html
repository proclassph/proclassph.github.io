First, converting R percent to r a decimal
r = R/100 = 7%/100 = 0.07 per year.

Solving our equation:
A = 33500(1 + (0.07 × 5)) = 45225
A = $45,225.00

The total amount accrued, principal plus interest, from simple interest on a principal of $33,500.00 at a rate of 7% per year for 5 years is $45,225.00.
