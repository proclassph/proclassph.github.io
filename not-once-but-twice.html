the probability of getting head once and tails one is 0.5

H = Heads
T = Tails

HH

2/4 = 1/2 = 0.5 = 50%

22. it occurs once in a minute, twice in a moment, and never in an hour.


ANSWER:

What occurs once in a minute, twice in a moment, and never in an hour?

The answer for this riddle is the letter "m"

The letter m occurs once in a minute (only 1 m)

The letter m occurs twice in a moment (there are 2 m's)

The letter m never occurs in an hour because there is no letter m in the word "hour"

Therefore the answer for the riddle "It occurs once in a minute, twice in a moment, and never in an hour" is the letter "M"
