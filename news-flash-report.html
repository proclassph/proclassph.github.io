2. A News Flash/ Weather Report is​


WHAT IS A NEWS FLASH?

A news flash is any piece of important reporting that often disrupts normal broadcasting schedule.This is usually done because the report is of high urgency and importance; usually, these are reports regarding natural disasters, emergency situations, and etc.

WHAT IS A WEATHER REPORT?

This is a systematic report regarding meteorological or atmospheric occurrences over a certain area.The first report is usually given by the national weather department of a nation and is then reported by other media sources such as news broadcasts.These weather reports usually span nationwide, being given by the country's national weather department.
